➕ Provision a New Patient
Creates a new patient record, generates a unique access token, and produces a delivery link you can share directly with the patient.
How it works
  1. Enter the patient's name or an anonymised identifier.
  2. A unique patientId and accessToken are generated locally.
  3. Share the delivery link or QR code with the patient.
  4. The patient opens the link — their PWA boots with their ID pre-loaded.
  5. Their check-ins appear in this dashboard automatically.
